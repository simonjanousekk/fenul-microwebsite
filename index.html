ABOUT

Fenul's design was inspired by an anatomy book, specifically the chapter on bones. You can easily spot their characteristic thinner centers and heavier ends. This serif typeface follows the same construction principles and also incorporates striking variations in weight and width. Italics will be available early next year.

Although Fenul was only available in beta version, designer Jana Papiernikova decided to submit the family for two awards. We are proud to announce that the project was awarded in both ADC Award and Visuelt.

Original design: Jana Papiernikova
Design: Viktor Mizera, Martin Vácha
Assistance: Marek Čuban
Spacing and Kerning: Igino Marini
First Sketch: 4/2021, Released: 10/2023
Range: 2 VF Axis: Weight, Width
